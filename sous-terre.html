In my house underground.

Find as many comrades as possible to play this game. Form a round and position one hand under your left neighbour and one hand above your right neighbour’s. Then sing the song (see below) by typing your turn in the left neighbor’s hand. We start with the youngest player. At the end of «one, two, free», you must succeed in removing your hand very quickly in order not to be eliminated. If the player who hits «Three» manages to hit his neighbour’s hand, he eliminates it. If he can’t, he slaps on his own hand and eliminates himself. The turn continues until there is a winner.

Lyrics of the song:
Dans ma maison sous te-erre
O ma wé ! O ma wé !
O téo téo ouistiti !
O téo téo ouistiti !
One two three !
