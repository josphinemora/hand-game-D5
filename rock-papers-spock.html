Rock, paper, scissors,
lizard, spock !

Find one or more comrades to play with you.
Each player must choose between: Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard or Spock. Each player keeps his selection secret at the beginning:
scissors cut the paper. The paper covers the rock. The rock crushes the lizard. The lizard poisons Spock. Spock crushes the scissors. The scissors behead the lizard. The lizard eats paper. The paper repels Spock. Spock vaporizes the rock. The rock crushes the scissors.
Players reveal their choice after counting in rhythm up to five by doing the rock sign with one hand. If there is a tie on a throw, players simply replay the point. In competition, the best players compete, with two, only one winner is chosen.
