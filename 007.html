007

Find a comrade to play with you. Position yourself face to face and get your hands out in front of you. The principle is to «kill» the opponent. You have to say the word «007» at the same time by tapping the rhythm on your thighs. Each player uses this rhythm to act simultaneously. You can choose between three actions:
reload the hands on the temples (allows to recover ammunition), protect the arms in cross in front of his chest (does not allow to die in case of shooting) and finally shoot hands in pistol towards the one you want to kill (which is impossible if no ammunition is available). Once your opponent is killed, the game starts again. If you kill yourself at the same time, the game cancels and you start the game again.
It’s up to you!
